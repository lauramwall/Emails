Whatever you’re doing — it’s working. So stick with it.

Here are a few tips to keep you going:

•

Focus on class assignments. Delegate daily tasks to family members who can take them on.
•

Find a great place to study. It may be your home office, nearby library or one of our local campuses.
•

Rely on your cheerleaders. Get support from close friends, family, advisors and more.
•

Apply coursework to office work. Put the skills and lessons you’re learning, to work for you.

Stay bold. Keep persevering. You’ve got this.
