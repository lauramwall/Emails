Here's what you need to know

In October 2017, you were notified that the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing updated its requirements for teacher certification and the corresponding Teaching Performance Assessment (TPA) you must complete.

The commission has issued additional guidance in alignment with Ed Code §44252.1 indicating you may continue in the teacher performance assessment you enrolled in.

What this means

If you’ve started CalTPA courses (TPA001 and TPA002): You’ll stay on your original CalTPA track. Your schedule will be updated as necessary to take TPA002, and you’ll proceed with completing CalTPA tasks one through four at the appropriate points in your program.

If you haven’t taken any CalTPA courses: You’ll be scheduled for the edTPA track (TPA100, TPA200 and edTPA submission during student teaching).
