Re: Introduction of Tuition Guarantee

Dear <<Key Contact Name>>:

At University of Phoenix, we’re always looking for new ways to bring greater value to our students and your employees.

I’m pleased to share in January we made a big change — we lowered our tuition and locked it in for students, including your employees. We’re calling it the University of Phoenix Tuition Guarantee.

This new simplified pricing program is designed to help students keep the same affordable tuition until they graduate from their program. Your employees will now receive one low, fixed tuition rate they can count on from their first class to their last. These new rates and the Tuition Guarantee went into effect January 17, 2018.

These new rates and the Tuition Guarantee went into effect January 17, 2018. Employees who enroll with University of Phoenix after this date will receive the new lower, locked‐in tuition. Rest assured, we’re offering this Tuition Guarantee to all of your employees who enrolled with us before January 17, 2018. They can choose to stay enrolled under the current terms of our Edcor agreement, or opt‐in to our new tuition guarantee program— whichever works best for them.

Edcor and University of Phoenix will also take care of employees who enroll under the University’s new Tuition Guarantee after the Cancellation Date of your agreement with the University of Phoenix. For these employees, we will waive resource fees for their first two courses each calendar year as part of our renegotiated educational partnership agreement with Edcor. This new benefit will replace the tuition reduction your employees were receiving under our <<Name of the Agreement>> dated <<Effective Date>> (Agreement) as we are cancelling the Agreement effective <<Days’ Notice>> days from the date of this letter ("Cancellation Date"). We’re working with Edcor to ensure a smooth transition of these services.

Please let your employees know about this great new benefit. We sincerely hope our new Tuition Guarantee program provides compelling reasons to continue to promote University of Phoenix as one of your preferred educational providers.
